FAQ

Questions, answered

Everything you need to know before starting your first role.

What if Ava picks the wrong people?
Ava doesn't make final decisions — she screens and ranks. You always review the shortlist and choose who to Advance or Pass. Think of her as a super-powered recruiter, not a replacement for your judgment.
How is my candidate data protected?
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. Candidate data is never shared with third parties.
Can I customize the screening questions?
Absolutely. Ava starts with smart defaults based on your role, but you can add, edit, or remove any question. You can also set custom scoring weights for the traits that matter most to your business.
What happens after the free trial?
Your first hire is always free, no matter what plan you're on. After that, you can upgrade to Growth or stay on Starter. No surprise charges, no locked-in contracts.
Do candidates need to download an app?
Nope. Candidates apply through a simple web link — no app install, no account creation. They take a quiz, answer voice questions, and they're done in under 10 minutes.
Can I integrate with my existing tools?
Yes. Hireflow connects with Slack, Google Calendar, Stripe, Notion, and Zapier. New integrations are added monthly based on customer requests.
